Creative learning

As experienced artists, makers and educators we provide creative ways to build self-esteem, confidence and respect through positive experiences.

We have a successful track record working on exciting and innovative arts projects with schools and community groups in West Sussex.

We are extremely resourceful and have heaps of enthusiasm, ideas and knowledge to work with you in your setting. We enable you to produce unique artwork, from personal pieces to large scale group projects.

We can work with all age groups and mixed abilities, encouraging inclusion and integration.

Fully insured and CRB checked.

Schools

We are dedicated to making creativity fun and educational for children.

Our proven skills as professional artists enable us to work with large or small groups of all abilities, from early years through to A-level.

Our aims are to:

  • Stimulate the imagination and offer new ways of expressing ideas
  • Realise potential through creativity
  • Improve self-confidence
  • Develop new skills
  • Encourage teamwork

Our schools workshops feature various mediums including: paint, textiles, recycled materials, clay, sculpture and drawing. All our workshops are tailormade to suit your needs or work with a current subject.

As Arts Award advisors we can lead individuals or groups with their chosen projects.

Please contact us for more information and see how we can inspire and motivate.
