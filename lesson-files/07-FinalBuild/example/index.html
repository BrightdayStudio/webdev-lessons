Cosy Coffee in Crawley

We are an independent coffee shop serving speciality coffee, home-made cakes and cookies, and all-day brunch on weekends, all in a cosy shop in the heart of historic Crawley. On Friday and Saturday nights we also offer cheese and meat platters with fine wines. We are new, so come say hello and meet our head of customer care - resident bulldog Arthur!

Full of beans

Our beans are roasted by our friends at the Climpson & Sons roastery in East London who source some of the best coffees in the world!

Munch
