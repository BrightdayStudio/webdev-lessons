Here is what's happening.

  • A Vintage Day on the Prom

    When: 29th September 2019, 11am - 3pm

    Where: East Beach Studios, Worthing

    Discover the rich history of Worthing's Promenade.

    Creative Waves invite you to put on your Sunday best and JOIN US at East Beach Studios for our VINTAGE DAY ON THE PROM. Recreate an outfit from any era, maybe Victorian, Edwardian, 1960s, '70s..... prizes for the best outfits.

    Everyone’s welcome. There will be FREE entertainment: Live music from Tim Keegan & friends, Pop up promenade theatre, Prof. Edwards Punch & Judy, Flea Circus...and more.

    Free Heritage walk & displays of Worthing's prom during WW2.

  • Pop Up on the Prom Heritage walk

    When: Tue, 13 August 2019, 10:30 – 12:00

    Where: On the Promenade (opposite Heene Road), Worthing. BN11 2QT

    Continuing our series of lighthearted heritage strolls along the Promenade, for this walk we're looking at the Victorians and their enthusiasm for all things 'seaside holiday'...however, all is not sunshine and roses in Sunny Worthing, as the last typhoid epidemic in Britain is about to ruin the fun, and riots are afoot amidst the goat rides and sea bathing... Come and find out what the problem was and why things got nasty between Warwick House and Greville Place. There will be singing and silly voices.

    Walks are open to all ages, and are part of the Heritage Lottery funded Pop Up on the Prom project by Creative Waves Community arts.
